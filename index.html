Things to do in ROURKELA

See the beauty of the city from the HILL TOP

One of the best places to see the nature at it's peak specially when you can see the sunrise in the winter.

KHANDADHAR WATERFALL

The Waterfall which is as high as 244m, which can take you to immense treking experience and also have the fun of taking bath at one of the coldest Mountain Water's

PANI PURI / PUCHKAS

Foodies can never ever live without Pani Puris or Puchkas as they call it. Rourkela serves one of the best pani puri that you can have in the entire region.
